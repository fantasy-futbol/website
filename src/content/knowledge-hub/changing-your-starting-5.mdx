**The Short Answer:** Go to My Team, toggle to next month, tap "Move" on a team, then tap "Here" where you want it to go. Changes are only allowed for future months, and they lock at 12:00 AM on the 1st.

---

## When Can You Change Your Lineup?

You can only change your lineup for **future months**.

| Month Status | Can You Change? |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Past month | ❌ No |
| Current month | ❌ No (already locked) |
| Next month | ✅ Yes (until it locks) |

Once a month begins, its lineup is locked. You're always looking ahead.

---

## The Deadline

Changes for next month must be made **before 12:00 AM on the 1st**.

| If You Want to Change... | Deadline |
|--------------------------|----------|
| January Starting 5 | December 31, 11:59 PM |
| February Starting 5 | January 31, 11:59 PM |
| March Starting 5 | February 28/29, 11:59 PM |
| April Starting 5 | March 31, 11:59 PM |

Miss the deadline? Your current lineup carries over unchanged.

→ *Full details: [The Monthly Lock](/knowledge-hub/the-monthly-lock)*

## How to Change Your Starting 5

### Step 1: Go to My Team

Tap the **My Team** tab in the bottom navigation.

### Step 2: Toggle to Next Month

At the top of the screen, you'll see the current month with arrows on either side. Tap the **right arrow** to move to the next month.

### Step 3: See the "Move" Buttons

When viewing a future month, each team row displays a **"Move"** button. These buttons only appear for future months — current and past months are locked.

### Step 4: Tap "Move" on a Team

Select the team you want to move. Once you tap "Move," all other teams' buttons change to **"Here"**.

### Step 5: Tap "Here" to Place the Team

Tap **"Here"** on the position where you want your selected team to go. **The two teams swap positions.**

### Step 6: Repeat as Needed

Continue moving teams until your Starting 5 and Bench are set the way you want them for next month.

## Example: Swapping a Starter and Bench Team

**Scenario:** You want to bench Napoli and start Frankfurt instead.

**Current lineup:**

| Starting 5 | Bench |
|------------|-------|
| Real Madrid | Frankfurt |
| Liverpool | Athletic Bilbao |
| Napoli | OG Nice |
| PSG | Real Sociedad |
| Atletico Madrid | Chelsea |

**Steps:**
1. Toggle to the future month
2. Tap **"Move"** on Napoli
3. Tap **"Here"** on Frankfurt

**Result:**

| Starting 5 | Bench |
|------------|-------|
| Real Madrid | Napoli |
| Liverpool | Athletic Bilbao |
| Frankfurt | OG Nice |
| PSG | Real Sociedad |
| Atletico Madrid | Chelsea |

Frankfurt is now a starter. Napoli is now on the bench.

## What You Can Do

### Swap a Starter with a Bench Team

The most common move. Promote a bench team to your Starting 5, demote a starter to the bench.

### Multiple Swaps

You can make as many moves as you want before the lock. Swap two teams, swap five teams, rearrange everything — it's your roster.

### Reorder Your Lineup

You can rearrange the order of your Starting 5 or Bench. Currently, order doesn't affect scoring — all 5 starters earn points equally.

---

## What You Can't Do

### Change the Current Month

Once the month begins, it's locked. You can only change future months.

### Add or Remove Teams

You can only swap positions between your existing 10 teams. You can't drop or add teams (waivers/trades are a future feature).

---

## When to Make Changes

### Ideal: A Few Days Before the Lock

Don't wait until the last minute. Give yourself time to review fixtures and think through your decisions.

### Risky: Last Minute

Life happens. Phones die. Last-minute changes are stressful and error-prone. Set your lineup early.

## Tips for Choosing Your Starting 5

- **Check fixture count** — How many matches does each team have?
- **Check fixture difficulty** — Who are they playing?
- **Check European football** — Teams in Champions League, Europa League, or Conference League have extra high-value matches
- **Check recent form** — Are they winning? Scoring?
- **Compare starters to bench** — Look at the PTS, GD, and MR columns for each team

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What if I don't make any changes?

Your current lineup carries over unchanged. If you're happy with your Starting 5, you don't have to do anything.

### Can I undo a move?

Yes — just move them back. The "Move" and "Here" system lets you swap teams as many times as you want before the lock.

### Do my changes save automatically?

Yes. Each swap is saved immediately. There's no "confirm" button for the overall lineup — moves are applied as you make them.

### Can other managers see my changes before the lock?

No. Your next month's lineup is private until the month begins.
