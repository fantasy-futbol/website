## You Just Finished the Draft. Now What?

Congratulations — you have a roster. Ten teams from across Europe's Big 5 leagues are now yours to manage for the season.

But the draft was just the beginning. Here's what happens next and what you should focus on in your first month.

---

## Your Initial Starting 5

**Your first 5 draft picks are automatically set as your Starting 5 for the first month.**

| Draft Pick | Role |

| Pick 1 | Starter |

This means your highest-drafted teams — presumably your best teams — are earning you points right away.

## First Month Checklist

### Week 1: Get Oriented

- [ ] **Explore the app.** Tap around. Find League Home, Table, My Team, and Info.
- [ ] **Check your Starting 5.** Go to My Team and confirm which teams are starting.
- [ ] **Look at upcoming fixtures.** See when your teams play and against whom.
- [ ] **Find your league's standings.** Check the Table tab to see the League Table.

### Week 2: Watch Your Teams

- [ ] **Track match results.** Check match cards after your teams play.
- [ ] **Understand the points.** See how GD and MRP combine on each match card.
- [ ] **Compare to your bench.** Note the faded points your bench teams are (not) earning you.
- [ ] **Watch the League Table.** See how you stack up against your leaguemates.

### Week 3: Start Planning

- [ ] **Pull up next month's fixtures.** Which of your teams have favorable schedules?
- [ ] **Compare starters vs. bench.** Should any bench teams be promoted?
- [ ] **Count matches.** Who has more games? Who has European football?
- [ ] **Identify your Starting 5 for Month 2.**

### Week 4: Execute

- [ ] **Make your lineup changes.** Swap starters and bench teams as needed.
- [ ] **Double-check before the lock.** Confirm your Starting 5 is correct.

## Key Screens to Know

### League Home

**What it shows:** Starting 5 rosters for all managers, plus match cards for all starter matches that day.

**Use it for:** Scouting opponents, seeing league-wide action, tracking the competition.

### Table

**What it shows:** Three tables — League Table (manager standings), Club Goal Differential, and Club Match Results.

**Use it for:** Checking who's winning, seeing which teams are performing, tracking your rank.

### My Team

**What it shows:** Your Starting 5 and Bench for the current month, plus match cards for all your teams.

**Use it for:** Managing your roster, tracking your teams' performance, planning lineup changes.

### Info

**What it shows:** Game rules, help articles, and resources (including this Knowledge Hub).

## What to Expect in Month 1

### Your Teams Will Play

Depending on the month, your Starting 5 might have anywhere from 15-40 combined matches. Each match earns (or loses) you points.

### You'll Make Mistakes

Everyone does. Maybe you'll realize your 4th pick has brutal fixtures while your 7th pick is cruising. That's what Month 2 is for.

### The League Table Will Shuffle

Early-season standings are volatile. Don't panic if you're not in first. Don't celebrate too hard if you are. It's a long season.

---

## Don't Worry About...

### Perfecting Your First Month

Your Starting 5 was auto-assigned. It's a reasonable default, but it might not be optimal. That's fine. You'll have 8+ more months to optimize.

### Falling Behind Early

One manager might jump out to a lead because their teams had favorable fixtures. The season is long. Gaps close.

### Understanding Everything Immediately

The scoring system, the competitions, the monthly lock — it's a lot. Give yourself time. By Month 2, you'll feel like a veteran.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change my Starting 5 during Month 1?

No. Once the month begins, your lineup is locked. But you can (and should) plan changes for Month 2.

### How do I know when the month ends?

The app shows a countdown to the next monthly lock. You'll also get notification reminders before the lock.

### Can I see my opponents' rosters?

Yes. The League Home page shows everyone's Starting 5. Rosters are public.

## Month 1 → Month 2 Transition

### Last Few Days of Month 1

1. Review your Starting 5's total points for the month
2. Check your bench teams' (missed) points
3. Look at Month 2 fixtures for all 10 teams
4. Decide your Month 2 Starting 5
5. Make the swaps in the app

### Lock Night (Last Day of Month 1)

1. Final check — confirm your lineup is correct
2. The lock hits at 12:00 AM on the 1st
3. Your Month 2 Starting 5 is now set
