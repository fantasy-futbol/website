**The Short Answer:** You either create a new league and invite friends to join, or you join an existing league using a League ID someone shared with you.

---

## Two Paths Into a League

When you finish creating your account, you'll see a simple choice:

1. **Create a League** — You start a new league and become the League Admin
2. **Join a League** — You enter a League ID to join a league someone else created

Most managers join leagues with friends, coworkers, or online communities. The game is designed around private leagues where you know (or get to know) your competition.

---

## Option 1: Create a League

### Who Should Create a League?

Create a league if:

- You want to organize a league among your friend group
- You're willing to be the League Admin (commissioner)
- You have at least 2 other people ready to join (3-manager minimum)

### How to Create a League

**Step 1: Tap "Create a League"**

From the "Do you have a league?" screen, select the create option.

**Step 2: Name Your League**

Enter a name for your league. This is what all managers will see.

Examples:
- "The Beautiful Game"
- "NYC Soccer Club"
- "Office FC"
- "College Roommates League"

Keep it clean — your leaguemates will see this all season.

**Step 3: You're In**

That's it. Your league is created, and you're automatically the **League Admin**.

You'll receive a **League ID** — a unique code that others use to join your league.

### What You Get as League Admin

When you create a league, you become the League Admin with special responsibilities:

- **Set the draft date and time**
- **Set the draft order**
- **Start the draft** when everyone's ready
- **Manage the league** throughout the season

### Your League ID

Every league has a unique **League ID** — a short code that looks something like:

```
482917
```

This is how other managers join your league. Share it with the people you want to play with.

---

## Option 2: Join a League

### Who Should Join a League?

Join a league if:

- Someone invited you to their league
- You have a League ID ready to enter
- You want to play but don't want to be the Admin

### How to Join a League

**Step 1: Get the League ID**

The League Admin (or any existing member) will share the League ID with you. It might come via text message, group chat, email, social media DM, or in person.

**Step 2: Tap "Join a League"**

From the "Do you have a league?" screen, select the join option.

**Step 3: Enter the League ID**

Type in the League ID exactly as it was shared with you.

**Step 4: You're In**

If the League ID is valid, you'll be added to the league immediately. You'll see the league name and any other managers who have already joined.

### What If the League ID Doesn't Work?

If you get an error:

- **Double-check the code.** Make sure you entered it exactly right — no extra spaces, correct numbers.
- **Ask the Admin.** They can confirm the correct League ID from their app.
- **Check if the league is full.** Leagues have a maximum of 12 managers. If it's full, you can't join.

## Inviting Friends to Your League

### How to Invite

**Option 1: Share the League ID directly**

Just send the League ID code to your friends via text, group chat, email, whatever. They enter it in the app to join.

**Option 2: Use the Invite Friends button**

The app has an "Invite Friends" button that opens your phone's native share sheet. This sends a pre-written message with your League ID and a link to download the app.

### What to Tell Your Friends

When inviting friends, let them know:

1. **Download the app** (if they haven't already)
2. **Create an account** with their email
3. **Use this League ID:** [your code]
4. **Be available for the draft** on [date/time]

## League Size

### Minimum: 3 Managers

You need at least 3 managers to run a league. With 98 teams available and 10 teams per roster, 3 managers means 30 teams drafted — leaving 68 undrafted.

### Maximum: 12 Managers

Leagues cap at 12 managers. With 12 managers drafting 10 teams each, that's 120 picks — but there are only 98 teams, so draft strategy becomes critical in larger leagues.

### Recommended: 4-8 Managers

| League Size | Teams Drafted | Teams Undrafted | Vibe |
|-------------|---------------|-----------------|------|
| 3 managers | 30 | 68 | Very deep rosters, easy waiver wire |
| 4 managers | 40 | 58 | Plenty of quality available |
| 5 managers | 50 | 48 | Balanced |
| 6 managers | 60 | 38 | Getting competitive |
| 8 managers | 80 | 18 | Tight, strategic drafting |
| 10 managers | 100 | 0* | Every team owned |
| 12 managers | 120 | 0* | Scarcity mode |

## Before the Draft

Once your league has enough managers, the League Admin needs to:

1. **Set the draft order** — Decide who picks first, second, etc.
2. **Schedule the draft** — Pick a date and time when everyone can attend
3. **Notify the league** — Make sure everyone knows when to show up

As a regular manager (non-Admin), your job is to:

1. **Confirm you can attend** the scheduled draft time
2. **Do your research** on teams before the draft
3. **Show up ready to pick**

See [The Draft Explained](/knowledge-hub/the-draft-explained) for everything you need to know about draft day.

## Can I Be in Multiple Leagues?

Yes. You can create or join multiple leagues with the same account.

Each league is completely separate — separate roster, separate draft, separate standings, separate competition.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I join a league after the draft?

No. All managers must be in the league before the draft begins. Once the draft starts, the league is locked.

### What if I don't know anyone playing Fantasy Fútbol?

A few options:

1. **Recruit your friends.** Send them the app link and sell them on the game.
2. **Join our Discord.** We have a community where solo managers can find leagues looking for members.
3. **Check Reddit.** The r/fantasyfutbol subreddit has league recruitment threads.

### What's the difference between League Admin and regular manager?

| Capability | League Admin | Regular Manager |
|------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Set draft date/time | ✅ | ❌ |
| Set draft order | ✅ | ❌ |
| Start the draft | ✅ | ❌ |
| Draft teams | ✅ | ✅ |
| Set Starting 5 | ✅ | ✅ |
| View league standings | ✅ | ✅ |
| Invite friends | ✅ | ✅ |

The Admin has a few extra controls, but gameplay is identical.
